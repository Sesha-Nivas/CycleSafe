# 🚀 Quick Start Guide - CycleSafe Navigation

## Step-by-Step Guide to Use Your Website

### 1️⃣ First Time Setup (Sign Up)

1. **Open the website** - You'll see the splash screen, then the login page
2. **Click "Sign Up"** at the bottom
3. **Fill in the form**:
   - Upload a profile photo (optional but recommended)
   - Enter your full name
   - Enter your email address
   - Create a password (minimum 6 characters)
   - Confirm your password
4. **Click "Create Account"**
5. ✅ You're now logged in!

### 2️⃣ Allow Location Access

When you first navigate to the map:
1. Browser will ask: **"Allow location access?"**
2. Click **"Allow"** or **"Yes"**
3. This is required for:
   - Showing your current location on the map
   - Live tracking during rides
   - Showing your location in the profile

### 3️⃣ Using the Navigate Section

**Search for a Destination:**
1. Click **"Navigate"** in the sidebar
2. You'll see a **real Google Map** with your current location (blue dot)
3. At the top, there's a **search bar**
4. **Type a destination** (e.g., "Central Park", "123 Main St", "Coffee shop")
5. **Select from suggestions** that appear
6. The map will show:
   - ✅ Your current location (blue marker)
   - ✅ The route (green line)
   - ✅ Destination marker
   - ✅ Route details on the right

**Before Searching:**
- Start Navigation button is **GRAY and DISABLED** ❌

**After Selecting Destination:**
- Start Navigation button is **GREEN and ENABLED** ✅

**Starting a Ride:**
1. Click **"Start Navigation"**
2. Watch your stats update in real-time:
   - Distance
   - Duration (timer)
   - Current Speed
   - Calories
3. Your location updates live on the map
4. The green route shows your path

**Stopping a Ride:**
1. Click **"Stop Riding"** (red button)
2. Your ride is automatically saved to the database
3. The map still shows your location
4. You'll see **"Last Trip"** details below the map
5. To search for a new destination, click the **X** button in the search bar

### 4️⃣ Home Dashboard

**Today's Stats:**
- Shows ONLY today's rides
- Displays:
  - Distance (km)
  - Duration (minutes)
  - Average Speed (km/h)
  - Calories Burned

**At Midnight (12:00 AM):**
- Stats automatically reset to 0
- Start fresh the next day!

**Safety Score:**
- Displayed at the bottom
- Higher = better riding behavior

### 5️⃣ Profile Page

**User Info:**
- Your profile photo, name, and email
- **Current Location** - automatically detected from GPS
- Click **"Refresh"** button to update all stats

**Overall Statistics (from ALL your rides):**
- Total Rides
- Total Distance (km)
- Badges Earned
- Average Speed (km/h)

**Achievements:**
Watch your progress bars grow!

1. **Century Rider** 🏆
   - Goal: Ride 100 km total
   - Your progress: Based on total distance
   - Example: 50 km = 50% complete

2. **Week Warrior** 📅
   - Goal: Complete 5 rides in the last 7 days
   - Your progress: Based on recent rides
   - Example: 3 rides this week = 60% complete

3. **Speed Demon** 🚴‍♂️💨
   - Goal: Reach 30 km/h average speed
   - Your progress: Based on overall avg speed
   - Example: 24 km/h avg = 80% complete

4. **Distance Master** 🎯
   - Goal: Complete a 50 km single ride
   - Your progress: Based on your longest ride
   - Example: 40 km longest = 80% complete

**When you reach 100% on any achievement:**
- 🎉 You earn a BADGE!
- 🏅 Badge counter increases
- 💯 100 points added to your total

**Member Metrics:**

**Member Status:**
- Bronze (0-4 badges): New cyclist
- Silver (5-9 badges): Experienced cyclist
- Gold (10+ badges): Elite cyclist

**Current Streak:**
- Days in a row with at least 1 ride
- Example: Ride Mon, Tue, Wed = 3-day streak
- Miss a day = streak resets to 0

**Total Points:**
- Formula: (Distance × 10) + (Rides × 50) + (Badges × 100)
- Example:
  - 75 km → 750 points
  - 12 rides → 600 points
  - 2 badges → 200 points
  - **Total = 1,550 points**

### 6️⃣ Safety Center

**Your Safety Score:**
- Based on your riding behavior
- 90-100 = Excellent
- 75-89 = Good
- Below 75 = Needs improvement

**Safety Leaderboard:**
- See ALL users ranked
- Sorted by:
  1. Safety Score (higher is better)

- Top 3 get special icons:
  - 🥇 1st place: Gold trophy
  - 🥈 2nd place: Silver medal
  - 🥉 3rd place: Bronze award

**Safety Tips:**
- Best practices for safe cycling
- Click any tip to learn more

### 8️⃣ Understanding Your Data

**Today's Stats vs Profile Stats:**
- **Home (Today's Stats)**: Only today's rides
- **Profile Stats**: ALL rides ever

**When do stats update?**
- ✅ Immediately after completing a ride
- ✅ Click Refresh in Profile to see latest
- ✅ Leaderboard updates in real-time

**What happens at midnight?**
- ✅ Today's stats reset to 0
- ✅ Profile stats stay the same (they're cumulative)
- ✅ Streaks check if you rode yesterday
- ✅ Week Warrior counts last 7 days

### 9️⃣ Troubleshooting

**Map not loading?**
- Check your internet connection
- Allow location permissions
- Refresh the page

**Location not accurate?**
- Make sure GPS is enabled on your device
- Try moving closer to a window (if indoors)
- Click Refresh in Profile

**Start Navigation button disabled?**
- Make sure you've searched and selected a destination
- The search must complete successfully
- Look for the route on the map

**Stats not updating?**
- Click the Refresh button in Profile
- Make sure you completed the ride (clicked Stop)
- Check that the ride was saved

**Today's stats showing old rides?**
- Check the date/time on your device
- Stats only show rides from current day
- Should reset automatically at midnight
